# Safe Chain - Style Guide

## Layout

- Mobile: 375px (Responsive Fluid)
- Desktop: 1440px +

## Colors

### Primary (Neon & Brand)

- Neon Purple: hsl(280, 100%, 50%) - *Used for buttons & glows*
- Royal Gold: #D4AF37 - *Used for accents*
- Cyan Blue: #2dcece - *Used for details*

### Backgrounds (Glassmorphism)

- Deep Space: linear-gradient(180deg, #180a29 0%, #050208 100%)
- Glass Panel: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.03) with Backdrop Blur

### Neutral

- White: hsl(0, 0%, 100%)
- Text Muted: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.7)

## Typography

### Font Family
- **Space Grotesk** (Google Fonts)
- Weights: 300, 400, 600, 700

### Icons
- Custom SVG & PNG assets
